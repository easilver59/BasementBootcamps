Collaborative Movement

Thousands of miles from home, Becca and Jenna met each other in the first semester of university in Scotland. Back in our hometown, Boston, we aspire to provide accessible, safe, and fun movement classes to all bodies.
Yoga and fitness are complementary practices. We hope to bridge the gap between these two movement regimens to improve your health and wellbeing. By participating in this holistic movement program, you will gain strength, flexibility, mobility, and a greater awareness of your mind and body.
Our joint program allows you to attend both modalities at the same price point; you can purchase class packs which you can apply to group fitness and/or yoga classes.
